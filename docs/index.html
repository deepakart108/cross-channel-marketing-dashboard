cpa spike · snapchat / Snap Ads - Prospecting · materiality 0.58
CPA rose 20% week-over-week, from $24.68 to $29.66.
A 20% week-over-week CPA jump on Snap Ads - Prospecting means you're paying more to acquire each result, and per Snap's own guidance this pattern is the platform-native sign of ad fatigue: 'over-indexing on impressions while ignoring frequency' causes performance to decay even while impression volume looks healthy, and rising CPA is exactly the downstream effect of that decay.
Next step: Pull paid frequency (paid impressions ÷ paid reach) and swipe rate for this campaign over the same week — if frequency climbed while reach stayed flat and swipe rate declined, that confirms the fatigue signature, and you should refresh creative or expand/exclude audience segments to restore fresh reach rather than adjusting bids alone.
