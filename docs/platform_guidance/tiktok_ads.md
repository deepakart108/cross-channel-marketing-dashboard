# TikTok Ads — Creative Fatigue & Refresh Cadence

Source: TikTok For Business Help Center — "Creative best practices for performance ads"
(ads.tiktok.com/help/article/creative-best-practices)

## When to refresh creative

TikTok recommends refreshing an ad group's creative "when delivery results exhibit a
consistently declining trend, or when daily new users are low" — a declining-delivery trend
combined with thinning new-audience reach is the platform's own definition of fatigue setting
in.

## Structure

- Run **3–5 different creatives per ad group** and **3–5 diversified ad groups per campaign**
  — enough variation that any single creative fatiguing doesn't stall the whole ad group.
- When refreshing, **add new creatives to the existing ad group** rather than spinning up a
  new ad group — this extends the ad group's learning/lifetime instead of resetting it.
- A weekly frequency of **2–3** is the safest starting benchmark for most campaigns; treat
  frequency climbing past that range alongside softening CTR as the fatigue signal, matching
  the "declining delivery results" warning sign above.

## Tooling

TikTok's Smart Creative automation is designed to detect and rotate fatigued creative
automatically (as early as 3–5 days into a campaign), and Video Insights is the tool for
diagnosing which specific creative is degrading.
